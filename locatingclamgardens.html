Locating Clam Gardens

Clam gardens are a highly visible archaeological feature that are still being used today. Since they are highly visible, they can be seen from satellite imaging, and therefore can be found using a program like google earth. You can download the program here for free and search for clam gardens yourself, however please remember that these features are important in many different ways, and should not be disrespected. Please exercise caution and be considerate of whose cultural property you are viewing. We hope that this will generate interest in clam gardens as well as awareness of the amount of clam gardens that exist on the coast.

Here is a map showing regions on the southern coast of British Columbia where clam gardens have been recorded. These regions have an abundance of known clam gardens due to concentrated research efforts, however, this map does not represent the full geographic range or knowledge of clam gardens on the Northwest Coast.
